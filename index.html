I also had my daughter during this time and finished my psychology degree as a student mother.

My background in Psychology has been a great foundation for raising my family, running a family-owned funeral home, and managing restaurants. However, it was frustrating to be surrounded by data everywhere without the means to scientifically analyze them. Now with my newly acquired programming and data analytics skills combined with my psychology background and customer service and management experience, my interest is in data visualization, data ethics and accesibility.
